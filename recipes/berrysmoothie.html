Berry Smoothie

A delicious berry smoothie that tastes great and stuff.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 3 ice blocks

Steps

  1. Blend blueberries, yogurt, orangue juice, sugar, vanilla extract and cinnamon together on a low speed for 30 seconds; increase speed to high and blend until smooth, about 2 minutes.
  2. Add ice and blend on high until smooth, about 1 minute more.
