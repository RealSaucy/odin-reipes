Taco Seasoning

Lrnhw2 make sick taco seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp chili powder.
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin.
  • 1 tsp salt.
  • 1/2 tsp ground paprika.
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder.
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder.
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes.
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano.

Steps

  1. Mix together chilli powder, cumin, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes and oregano in a small bowl.
